Our Story

A Legacy Cast in Stone

Designed in the 1960s with Frank Lloyd Wright's organic modernist touch, Samarkand House is built around two massive sandstone boulders — architecture that feels grown from the earth rather than placed upon it.

Just 50 minutes from Islamabad yet worlds apart, this former family retreat takes its name from the ancient Silk Road city of Samarkand, evoking a spirit of history, fusion, and enchantment.

We host just one group at a time. Your stay is private, curated, and entirely yours.

"Where luxury meets nature, stories are shared over meals, and time slows in the embrace of the land."

Samarkand House is booked exclusively — one group at a time, for the entire property. The nightly rate shown is for The Main House, which sleeps up to 10 guests across 4 bedrooms and a bunk room, with full use of the grounds, gardens, and pool.

The Cottage and The Annex are available as add-ons if your group needs extra space. Add them when selecting your dates and the total will update automatically.

Peak season runs March through October. Weekend rates apply Thursday through Sunday. All other nights are charged at the weekday rate. Your exact total is calculated night by night when you select your dates.

A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance due before check-in. A 16% tax applies to all bookings. For custom packages, extended stays, or event pricing, please get in touch via WhatsApp.
